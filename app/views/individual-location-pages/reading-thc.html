Reading Tribunal Hearing Centre

Types of cases heard: Employment

An tribunal is responsible for hearing claims from people who think someone such as an employer or potential employer has treated them unlawfully.

Examples of unlawful treatment claims that we may hear include:

  • unfair dismissal
  • discrimination
  • unfair deductions from pay
