Reading Crown Court

Types of cases heard: Crime

A Crown Court is a criminal court and deals with serious crimes such a murder, drug trafficking, rape and robbery. I also deals with appeals against a magistrates’ court conviction. Also a case may be passed from a magistrates court for sentencing.

A Crown Court normally has a jury and a judge who decides what sentence you will get.
