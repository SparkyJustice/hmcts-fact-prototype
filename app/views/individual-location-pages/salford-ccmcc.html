County Court Money Claims Centre

Types of cases heard: Money Claims

County Court Money Claims Centre (CCMCC) is a central small claims court that has been set up to manage and process all money claims up to £10,000 and the fast track service will cover cases between £10,000 and £25,000.

The CCMCC is a national service. Enquires can be made by phone or email. Claims can be made on online or in writing. Your local court is no longer able to accept claims forms.
