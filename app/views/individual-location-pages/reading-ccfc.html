Reading County Court and Family Court

Types of cases heard: Adoption, Bankruptsy, Children, Divorce, Domestic violence, Forced Marriage and FGM, High Court District Registry, Housing Possession, Money Claims

A County Court deals with civil law cases - so disputes between two parties for example over a property, over a contract or over a personal injury. It does not handle criminal cases. Most matters are decided by a district judge or a circuit judge. There are no juries.

A Family Court helps families avoid disputes or if they should arise to resolve those problems quickly.
